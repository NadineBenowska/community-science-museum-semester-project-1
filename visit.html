Visit Us

Location

The museum is located at Museumsveien 22 3540 Nesbyen

Opening Hours

Monday-Tuesday: Closed. Wednesday-Friday: 10-17 Saturday-Sunday:10-15

Admission

The entrance is Free for all. Guided tours leaves every hour at the main entrance.

Accessibility

The museum has accessibility ramps, audio guides, eleveators and braille display signs.

Cafe

Cafe is located on the 1st floor and provides lunches, drinks, coffe, snacks and more.

Gift Shop

Gift shop is located on the 1st floor. Our shop offers great gifts, memorabilia and other.
