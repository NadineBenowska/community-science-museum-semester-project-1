Events

Sensory Friendly Sundays

Sensory Friendly Sunday is a monthly event designed for kids, teens, and adults with sensory processing differences, autism spectrum disorder, or developmental disabilities.

Night in the Museum

Why not visit us after dark? Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays.

Maker Space

Here you can be an inventor or artist every day. Make a giant arm with which you can give a present at 1.5 meters. Here you will find many fun activities

Sensational Science

Sensational Science is about all these things we encounter every day. Here you have the chance to create these phenomena for yourself and to see how they work

Energetica

Experience the energy captured from wind, water and the sun, and battle the forces of nature. Energetica consists of sculptures and installations.

Hands-on Chemistry

In the chemistry laboratory, cisitors can become scientists in their own right- for a little while at least. Lab coat? Check! Safety glasses? Check!
